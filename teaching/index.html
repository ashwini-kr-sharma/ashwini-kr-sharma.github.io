Research supervision

I have experience in mentoring and supervising young researchers. I have often come up with my own research proposals and pursued such projects successfully with junior researchers as their bachelor/master thesis or internship projects. Also, I have assisted in the onboarding process of new PhDs and PostDocs helping them start their research projects, learn new techniques and establish themselves in the group.
